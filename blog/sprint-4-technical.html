- In both cases:

- What was the problem?

The problem was my panicked state, consuming my brain power and lessening my thinking abilities.

- What problem solving techniques did you use?

Google, getting up to get some fresh air, and coming back to the problem. Asking peers helped, too.

- How did you feel throughout the process?

Initial annoyance, panic, frustration, then finally, resolve or fed up enough to want to resolve things. Or I calm myself down enough to go through the steps one by one again.

- What did you learn?

Being stressed is a constant state for me and I need to find a way to manage it. Because when I'm less stressed, I'm more able to solve a problem that may otherwise frustrate me.
