Reflection: Core Learnings

- What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

I have learned that I am harsh on myself and I need to work on giving myself credit in regards to the capacity I have for learning. I've learned that my time management will be a crucial factor in my success or lack thereof in the course. I will need to come up with better planning for the future not just for this bootcamp but in my everyday life.

- What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

How integral it has been to my well-being and learning. I would appreciate more topics on how to to unlock my potential, such as the subject on neuroplasticity.

- What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

Applying them and using them to further my learning. They are useful but need more time to be processed and put into practice.

- Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?

Because the industry needs more dynamic individuals who'd work well in group settings as well as being well-adjusted to the pressures of adapting with the ever changing tech-landscape. And also because this is an awesome philosophy of learning in general, which I am immensely grateful for! It's also a great break from the monotony of the staring at the screen in frustion.

- Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practicing programming instead? Justify your answer.

Quite the opposite. They are my FAVOURITE topics. They've helped me grow and provided encouragement. Most valuable resources by far besides the bean bags.
